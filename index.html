The Format

The Only League That Plays Like This.

4-man teams. Split matchups across the entire course. Three formats in one round. Every player locked in for all 18 holes.

I
Holes 1–6
BEST BALL

Each player plays their own ball. Best score per hole counts for the team. Cover your partner, manage your game, and let the better ball do the work.

II
Holes 7–12
ALT SHOT

One ball. Two players. Alternate every stroke. This is where partnerships are forged or broken — every shot carries your partner's round. No room to freelance.

III
Holes 13–18
INDIVIDUAL

Every player. Every ball. Every score counts personally. The back six is yours alone — and every stroke follows you to the Finals leaderboard.

Membership

Two Ways In. One League.

Membership is capped — field size is determined by course cart capacity, not an arbitrary number. That keeps the league tight, the competition real, and every spot meaningful. Active members hold a permanent team roster spot. Reserve members are the depth that keeps the league running.

The Season & Finals

Every Round Builds The Purse.

Twenty-four tournaments across the Gulf Coast corridor — shotgun starts on the best courses between Panama City Beach and Destin. Venues are being finalized now.

Every tournament pays out to the top 5 teams. A skins game runs alongside — money on every hole across all three format phases. All scoring is net. Points accumulate all season, and the top teams by standings carry into the Finals where the season purse is on the line.
