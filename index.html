The Format

Three Formats. One Round. No Passengers.

4-man teams split across the course. Teammates ride together — opponents in the adjacent cart. Every player locked in for all 18 holes across three rotating formats. The pressure shifts every six holes. Nobody checks out.

I
Holes 1–6
BEST BALL

Each player plays their own ball. Best score per hole counts for the team. Cover your partner, manage your game, and let the better ball do the work.

II
Holes 7–12
ALT SHOT

One ball. Two players. Alternate every stroke. This is where partnerships are forged or broken — every shot carries your partner's round. No room to freelance.

III
Holes 13–18
INDIVIDUAL

Every player. Every ball. Every score counts personally. The back six is yours alone — and every stroke follows you to the Finals leaderboard.

The Format
HOW IT WORKS

4-man teams. Split matchups across the course. Teammates ride together — opponents in the adjacent cart. Both cart groups play the same match simultaneously on opposite sides of the course and converge mid-round.

Holes 1–6
BEST BALL

Best net score per hole counts for the team against the full field.

Holes 7–12
ALT SHOT

One ball, two players. Teammates alternate every stroke.

Holes 13–18
INDIVIDUAL

Every player, every ball. Scores count personally toward standings.

The Skins Game
MONEY ON EVERY HOLE

Optional buy-in. The skins competition runs across the entire field — not just your matched opponent. Format changes every six holes. Ties carry over within each phase. New phase always starts fresh. Winners receive pro shop vouchers — no cash pot.

Holes 1–6
BEST BALL SKINS
Split 4 Ways

Lowest net best ball score in the field wins the skin.

Holes 7–12
ALT SHOT SKINS
Split 2 Ways

Lowest net alt shot score among all cart groups wins.

Holes 13–18
INDIVIDUAL SKINS
Winner Takes All

Every player vs. the whole field. The real money.

HANDICAP RULES

2

4

5
Balanced Team Assignment — Teams assigned to ensure a balanced mix across the 0–18 index spectrum.
