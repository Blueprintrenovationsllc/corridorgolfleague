01 — The Format

How It Works

4-man teams. Split matchups across the course. Teammates ride together — opponents in the adjacent cart. Both cart groups play simultaneously on opposite sides of the course and converge mid-round. Every player locked in for all 18 holes.

Holes 1–6

BEST BALL

Best net score per hole counts for the team against the full field.

Holes 7–12

ALT SHOT

One ball, two players. Teammates alternate every stroke.

Holes 13–18

INDIVIDUAL

Every player, every ball. Scores count personally toward standings.

02 — The Skins Game

Money on Every Hole

Optional buy-in. Skins run across the entire field — not just your matched opponent. Format shifts every six holes. Ties carry over within each phase. New phase always starts fresh.

Holes 1–6

BEST BALL SKINS

Split 4 Ways

Lowest net best ball score in the field wins the skin.

Holes 7–12

ALT SHOT SKINS

Split 2 Ways

Lowest net alt shot score among all cart groups wins.

Holes 13–18

INDIVIDUAL SKINS

Winner Takes All

Every player vs. the whole field. The real money.

05 — Eligibility & Fair Play

Handicap Rules

1
 GHIN Verified Index Required — Active USGA handicap index required. Self-reported handicaps not accepted. ~$30–40/year through your home club or USGA.
2
 Maximum Index: 18 — Open to players with a verified GHIN index of 0–18.
3
 Net Scoring Throughout — All tournament scoring, skins, and standings calculated on a net basis.
4
 Sandbagging Clause — Consistent over-performance triggers a mandatory handicap review. The league reserves the right to adjust playing handicaps.
5
 Balanced Team Assignment — Teams assigned to ensure a balanced mix across the 0–18 index spectrum.
