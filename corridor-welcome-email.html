01 — The Format

Three Formats. One Round. No Passengers.

4-man teams compete in a split matchup format. Teammates ride together — opponents in the adjacent cart. Both cart groups play simultaneously on opposite sides of the course and converge mid-round. Every player locked in for all 18 holes. The pressure shifts every six holes. Nobody checks out.

Holes 1–6

BEST BALL

Best net score per hole counts for the team against the full field.

Holes 7–12

ALT SHOT

One ball, two players. Teammates alternate every stroke. Chemistry wins here.

Holes 13–18

INDIVIDUAL

Every player, every ball. Scores count personally toward standings and the Corridor Cup.

02 — Team Structure

Your Team. Your Name. Your Identity.

Every team carries 6 members — 4 active players and 2 reserves. Teams are formed by friends. Each team chooses their own name and identity. A captain manages the lineup each week and submits the playing 4 to the league before each tournament.

Active Players

4 PER ROUND

The four competing that week. Must meet the combined handicap requirement of 20 at 50%.

Team Reserves

2 ON ROSTER

Part of the team. Called up when a player can't make it. Full tournament fee when playing. Spectator access when not.

Leadership

1 CAPTAIN

Confirms attendance, manages lineup, and is the league's point of contact for the team.

Shorthanded Rule

If a full lineup isn't available a team may play shorthanded or borrow a reserve from another team with captain approval. Borrowed reserves play under their own handicap for that tournament only.

03 — Eligibility & Fair Play

Handicap Rules

All scoring is net at 50% of your GHIN index across all 18 holes. Strokes are distributed by the course stroke index card — wherever the hardest holes fall across the three formats is where your strokes land. BlueGolf handles all calculations automatically per course.

1
GHIN Verified Index Required — Active USGA handicap index required. ~$30–40/year through your home club or USGA.
2
Maximum Index: 15 — Plus Handicaps Welcome — Open to players with a verified GHIN index of + to 15. Protects the competitive standard and pace of play.
3
50% Handicap Allowance — All net scoring at 50% of GHIN index across 18 holes. A 15 plays as 7 or 8. A 10 plays as 5. Scratch plays as scratch. Plus handicaps play to their plus allowance.
4
Team Composition — Minimum Combined 20 — The playing 4 must have a minimum combined handicap of 20 at 50%. Prevents stacking and keeps competition balanced.
5
Sandbagging Clause — Consistent net over-performance triggers a mandatory handicap review. The league reserves the right to adjust playing handicaps.

04 — Skins Competition

Pro Shop Vouchers Every Round

Optional buy-in. Skins run across the entire field — not just your matched opponent. Winners receive pro shop vouchers paid out at end of round. Same 50% handicap allowance applies. Ties carry within each phase — each new phase starts fresh.

Holes 1–6

BEST BALL SKINS

Split 4 Ways

Lowest net best ball in the field wins the skin.

Holes 7–12

ALT SHOT SKINS

Split 2 Ways

Lowest net alt shot among all cart groups wins.

Holes 13–18

INDIVIDUAL SKINS

Winner Takes All

Every player vs. the whole field. Biggest vouchers of the round.
